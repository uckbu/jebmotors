Our Story

J.E.B. Motors was founded by John Balci, with the goal of continuing his high-school work on the creation of a low-speed vehicle outside the bounds of secondary school.

Our Cars

Autonomous LSV

The Autonomous LSV is a fully-electric vehicle aiming to achieve a low-speed delegation, such that it is federally regulated to drive on highways where the speed limit does not exceed 35 mph.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate an understanding of the design process, prepare for bigger steps, and to create a cheaper car for those who are uninterested in traversing the dangerous American highway.

John Balci

John Balci is the founder and CEO of J.E.B. Motors.

A high school student at TJHSST, he plans to continue his strives in innovation past the bounds of high school, through graduation.

He began to fuse his capabilities in engineering and the arts, allowing him to cover the external as well as the internal design of his vehicles.

Technology

We integrate sensor-driven technology, electric powertrains, and sustainability into vehicles to create an end-product that is not only useful to the consumer, but beneficial to the environment and safety of others.

Components and Their Uses

Component Purpose Description
Montenergy ME1718 Sealed Brushless Motor (x2) Motor. Converts electrical energy into mechanical motion to drive the vehicle. This motor provides 100 amps at 48VDC continuously (4.8 kW), with a 2-minute surge power up to 300 amps (14.4 kW), operating at approximately 3500 RPM. It drives the vehicle by converting electrical energy into mechanical motion, and while rated up to 60VDC, it is capped at 48VDC to match the controller's capacity.
Curtis 1232 SE 3-Phase Induction Motor Controller (x2) Converts DC power into modulated 3-phase AC power to control the motor. The controller delivers 175 amps at 48VDC continuously (8.4 kW), with a surge power of up to 350 amps (16.8 kW) for 2 minutes. It enables precise control over vehicle performance by converting battery power into the necessary AC power.
Powerizer 12.8V 40Ah LiFePO4 Battery Packs Stores electrical power and supplies it to the vehicle's powertrain. These lithium iron phosphate batteries are wired in a series-parallel configuration to provide 48VDC to the powertrain. Each battery offers 512 watt-hours, making them highly efficient and stable, delivering instant power without the need for cooling.
Battery Management System (BMS) Ensures the safe operation and balance of the battery packs. The BMS monitors voltage balance, charging, and discharging processes to maintain the health and efficiency of the battery packs.
Briggs & Stratton 206 Crate Engine Provides internal combustion power when needed. This internal combustion engine produces 8.8 horsepower and 13.56 Nm of torque at 6100 RPM, offering additional power when necessary.
Curtis Type 1204-412 Brushed DC Motor Controller Regulates power to a brushed DC motor. This controller ensures smooth speed and torque control for a brushed DC motor.
